Welcome to COMPROT.

What is COMPROT? - COMPROT is an exhaustive resource of protein-protein and drug-protein interactions with the aim of providing a uniform data set for drug repositioning and further analysis. Our database COMPROT contains three different types of entities: drugs, proteins as well as relations between them.

How do I explore COMPROT? - You can search for drugs, proteins or bindings by using one of the corresbonding links above or you can use the fulltext search as well.
