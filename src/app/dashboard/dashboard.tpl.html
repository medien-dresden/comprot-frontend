Welcome to COMPROT.



What is COMPROT? - COMPROT provides target-compound and compound-target interactions for further analysis. Our database COMPROT contains three different types of entities: compounds, targets as well as relations between them.

How do I explore COMPROT? - You can search for compounds and targets by using the search box above and save them to your own workbench. Click one of the corresponding links above to get to your compound- or target-workbench.
